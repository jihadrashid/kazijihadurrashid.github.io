Assessment of Future Changes in Irrigation Demand of Boro Rice in Brahmaputra-Jamuna Floodplain of Bangladesh Using Observed and Model Data

My Role

  • Data Processing & Analysis
  • Data Visualization using Maps and Graphs

Tools Used

  • R Programming (dplyer & xts Packages)
  • ET0 calculator
  • CropWat
  • ArcGIS

Method Used

  • Calculation of reference evapotranspiration (ET0) and crop evapotranspiration (ETc)
  • Calculation of water required for land preparation
  • Percolation and seepage losses calculation of water from paddy field
  • Calculating effective precipitation
  • Total irrigation demand calculation using FAO-56 model
  • Spatial analysis of the climate change trends and irrigation demand using GIS modeling
